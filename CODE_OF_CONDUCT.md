# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

By participating in this project, you agree to abide by its terms.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to: **security@jugaar.llc**

Reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. All community leaders are obligated to respect the privacy and security of the reporter.

## Summary

We are committed to making participation in this project a welcoming experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity. We expect all participants to communicate respectfully, accept constructive feedback gracefully, and focus on what is best for the community.

For the full text of our code of conduct, please visit:
https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/
